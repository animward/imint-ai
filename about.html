About IMINT

IMINT is the Imagery Intelligence platform operated by the EGEX Intelligence Division. The platform provides tools and methodologies for the collection, processing, and analysis of visual intelligence data from multiple sources.

Platform Capabilities:

  • Multi-Spectrum Satellite Analysis — Processing and interpretation of satellite imagery across visible, infrared, and synthetic aperture radar bands. Change detection algorithms identify temporal variations across monitored areas of interest.
  • AI-Driven Pattern Recognition — Machine learning pipelines trained on geospatial datasets for automated feature extraction, anomaly detection, and pattern classification across large-area imagery.
  • Geospatial Information Systems — Coordinate mapping, terrain classification, elevation modeling, and infrastructure monitoring integrated with standard GIS frameworks.
  • Temporal Change Detection — Automated comparison of multi-temporal imagery for infrastructure changes, environmental shifts, and activity pattern analysis.
  • Terrain Analysis — Digital elevation models, slope analysis, viewshed computation, and terrain classification for operational planning and assessment.
  • Infrastructure Monitoring — Continuous automated monitoring of critical infrastructure with anomaly alerting and change reporting.

IMINT integrates with the broader EGEX Intelligence Division toolset including ANONSEC (security research), the Intelligence Analysis Archive (cia-us.com), and the OSINT collection pipeline. Platform access and capability inquiries: agent@egex.ai
